What We Offer

We are first and foremost a cigar lounge! We offer a wide selection of premium cigars and our staff is dedicated to providing you with a premium cigar smoking experience. Psst! If you're a member, we offer a selection of amazing products, sales, and even personal humidors which you can access with the push of a button. Check it out in our Member's Page. We'd love to personalize your experience even further.

If two things were ever meant to go together, it's Cigars and Bourbon! In keeping with that spirit (no pun intended) we offer a variety of bourbons to enjoy. Take yourself on a journey of tastes and aromas. Tell us what your favorite combos are. We'd love to hear about them and offer them to others.

Our goal is to provide a relaxed environemt and the classic way to do that is through the beautiful tunes of Smooth Jazz. However, we've been known to get a little upbeat from time-to-time. Check out our Events section to see when you can catch the next live show.

What kind of lounge would we be if we didn't have the hottest sports events queued up and ready to watch? We offer a list of amenities for the avid sports fan. Including private watch party rooms that you can reserve for you and all of your friends which include catering options from other local business around Columbus.
